# 📖 Project Overview

The **Bling Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze coffee sales performance across multiple store locations, product categories, and time periods.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using KPIs, interactive visualizations, and dynamic filters. It helps stakeholders monitor sales performance, identify top-performing products, compare store performance, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

Develop an interactive sales analytics dashboard that provides management with a centralized view of sales performance across stores, products, and time periods, enabling data-driven decisions for revenue growth and operational efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Expected Outcomes

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Compare sales across store locations
- Track monthly sales trends
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Improve inventory planning
- Support pricing and promotional strategies
- Enable faster business decision-making

# 📊 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Cards

Provides a quick summary of:

- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Total Stores
- Total Products
- Total Transactions
- Highest Store Revenue

---

### Sales by Product Category

Visual: **Line Chart**

Shows sales performance across different product categories.

Business Value:

- Identify top-selling products
- Understand category performance
- Support inventory planning

---

### Sales by Store Location

Visual: **Donut Chart**

Compares sales generated by:

- Tampa
- Miami
- Orlando

Business Value:

- Compare regional performance
- Allocate marketing budgets
- Improve operational planning

---

### Monthly Sales Trend

Visual: **Bar Chart**

Displays sales by month to identify:

- Seasonal demand
- Peak sales periods
- Sales growth trends

---

### Product Performance Table

Displays:

- Product Category
- Total Sales
- Sales Percentage
- Sales Ranking
- Category Status

Business Value:

- Identify best-performing categories
- Support pricing decisions
- Improve product strategy

---

### Interactive Filters

Users can filter the dashboard by:

- Product Category
- Quarter
- Sales Metric

---

# 📌 Key Insights

- Coffee is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Tea is the second highest-performing product.
- Tampa generated the highest sales among all stores.
- Monthly sales show an increasing trend during later months.
- Low-performing categories can be targeted with promotional campaigns.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for Coffee and Tea.
- Promote lower-performing product categories.
- Replicate successful strategies from the Tampa store.
- Use monthly sales trends for demand forecasting.
- Monitor product rankings regularly.
